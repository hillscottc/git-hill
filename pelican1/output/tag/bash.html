emacs-mode bash readline

C-a Move to the beginning of line.
C-e Move to the end of line.

M-f Move forward a word.
M-b Move backward a word.

C-k kill-line | Kill text from point to end of line.
C-u Kill backward to beginning of line.
M-d kill-word | Kill to the end of the current word.
C-w Kill the word behind.
C-y yank | Yank the top of the kill ring.
M-y yank-pop | Rotate the kill ring, yank the new top. |
